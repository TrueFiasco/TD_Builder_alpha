Summary
[edit]

graphPlot creates 2D graphs with data points or curves plotted over XY grids with controls over units, ranges, divisions and labels.

NOTE: See https://derivative.ca/community-post/asset/graphplot-comp-2d-graph-plotter/63757 for more discussion and other versions.

Setup is through parameters, and you can input data via the CHOP, SOP and DAT inputs.

First choose your overall panel size, then connect some data to it, then adjust the custom parameters to fit tha data on the graph to your liking.
