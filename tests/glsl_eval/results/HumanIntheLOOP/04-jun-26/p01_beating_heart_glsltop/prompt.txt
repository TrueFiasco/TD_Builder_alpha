3D render of a beating heart with controllable uniforms (heart_phase, beat_amplitude). As close to a functioning four-chambered human heart: RA/RV/LA/LV, septum, valves (tricuspid/pulmonary/mitral/aortic); wall layers epicardium/myocardium/endocardium; pulmonary + systemic circuits. Slice plane via 3 clamping vec2 uniforms for x/y/z cardinal directions. 2 vec4 rxyz to rotate heart and camera, 4th value = heart scale and camera FOV. Beautiful photorealistic render with studio-quality lighting.
